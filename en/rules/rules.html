0. Main Rules

0.1. All players must respect each other. Insults, abuses and toxic behaviour are forbidden.

0.2. Use of cheats, mods or any other software that gives you advantage over other players is forbidden (except for X-Ray and Baritone).

0.3. Robbery of resources, items or any other property of other players is forbidden.

1. Forts and Sieges Rules

1.1. Use of TNT and similar explosive materials for purpose of damaging forts is forbidden. The only allowed equipment to destroy walls are cannons, catapults, trebuchets and similar siege equipment.

1.2. Any fort protection mechanisms and equipment must be realistical enough and comply with medieval vibe of the server.

2. Combat Rules

2.1. Players can attack forts only after informing and warning owner of the fort (important: you must ping the owner in discord server of flopia).

2.2. Sieges must be conducted in time and rules that have both sides have agreed to.

2.3. Combat actions (such as sieges and battles) can be conducted only when both sides are online.

3. Dragons Use Rules

3.1. Dragons are forbidden to destroy armies (on the move, in combat or in idle state) and destroy structures, buildings and cities of other players.

3.2. Murder of dragons by players other than owner is forbidden.

4. Architectury and Economics Rules

4.1. All forts, castles and similar buildings should be built in medieval style. Please remember about the medieval vibe of the server.

4.2. All Players should use realistical methods of earning money and conducting trades. The use of bugs (dupes and similar exploits) is explicitly forbidden.
